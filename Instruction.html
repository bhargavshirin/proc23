Instructions

The following rules and regulations apply to all students. The goal of this platform is to provide a safe and secure environment for all students to learn and grow.

Requirements :


  • Students must have a stable internet connection to access the exam platform.(Min 100kb/s)
  • Students must have a webcam to access the exam platform.
  • Students must have a microphone to access the exam platform.
  • Students must required a silent place to write the exam.
  • Students must have a computer or Moblile or Tablet to access the exam platform.
  • Students must have a valid ID (Aadhaar Card, Student ID) to access the exam .
  • Students must have a valid phone number to access the exam platform.

Proctoring Related:


  • Your face will be verified before the start of the assessment from the photo that was captured during registration
  • Students must not use any other device while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other browser while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other tab while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other application while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other person while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other calculator while writing the exam.
  • Students must not use any other mobile while writing the exam.
